Andromachi Tsoukala

Most recently, the research I conducted for my PhD focused on predictive reading comprehension in first and second language adult speakers of English. I used behavioural methods, such as self-paced reading and eye-tracking, in combination with cognitive testing. You can find out more about my PhD research here: https://doi.org/10.17863/CAM.104437

I have also worked on several projects involving either young children (typical and atypical development such as ASD) or older adults that present with impairments in language and cogntion (aphasia, dementia and schizophrenia).

  • AHRC-funded project (ongoing): Language and Mental Health (Role: Collaborator, working with Prof Wolfram Hinzen at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, and Dr Vitor Zimmerer and Prof Rosemary Varley at University College London, among other collaborators).
